## Overview
This project involved sustainability and ESG-focused analytics work conducted in collaboration
with EssilorLuxottica. The objective was to analyze environmental and social metrics from public
disclosures and internal summaries to support benchmarking, reporting, and decision-making.

Due to confidentiality, datasets, code, and internal documents are not publicly available.

## Scope of Work
- Cleaned and structured ESG-related datasets derived from sustainability disclosures
- Standardized environmental and social metrics for cross-period comparison
- Analyzed trends and gaps in sustainability performance
- Created summary tables and visual outputs for stakeholder review
- Translated technical findings into clear, non-technical insights

## Methodology
- Data preprocessing and cleaning using Python and Excel
- Metric alignment across reporting periods
- Exploratory analysis to identify patterns and inconsistencies
- Synthesis of insights into concise written summaries and visuals

## Results & Impact
- Improved clarity and consistency of ESG metrics used for internal benchmarking
- Highlighted key environmental and social performance trends
- Supported sustainability reporting and strategic discussions
